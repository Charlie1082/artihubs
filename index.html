From signal to conversation

Skill and trust belong in the creation itself. Opportunity and assurance should not be searched for in view counts or algorithms.

Maker signal

Write your Maker Tag. Record the story of your idea, tools, lead time, and collaboration. Artihubs will keep studying how to support the complex process from multiple angles. We look forward to your great work.

Finder journey

Find what you need. Find the Maker who can help solve the problem, then write the unresolved issue or what you are looking for in the Request Tag. Leave the rest for Artihubs to work through. Do not be intimidated by complex procedures or paperwork. We support your great journey.

Artihubs mission

We study how Makers and Finders can come closer. When there is an idea and a discovery, our work is to reduce unnecessary cold DMs, complex explanations, sample requests, delivery notes, receipts, and other documents by providing forms and automatic drafting functions in the platform.

Trust and assurance

This is not a journey for casual networking. Between the time and cost you spend, trust is required. Artihubs will keep studying procedure logs, document drafting and records, and process logging systems so your ideas, time, and cost can be protected through a trustworthy process.
